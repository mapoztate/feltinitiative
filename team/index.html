MEET THE TEAM.

Angelina Li

Executive Director

Angelina Li is a high school junior from Cherry Hill, New Jersey. She is both the founder and executive director of Felt Initiative. Through Felt Initiative, Angelina strives to create a platform in which native English-speaking students can get the chance to guide a foreign student —one-on-one— in mastering a new language along her team. Besides leading Felt Initiative, Angelina leads the volunteer team at her local Chinese school, founded a hospital-based volunteer club at her high school, plays volleyball for her school team, and takes part in a variety of interest-based clubs at her high school. When she’s not working on Felt Initiative, you can find her exploring new hotspots with friends and family, trying out savory dishes or traditional snacks like the self-proclaimed food connoisseur that she is, or enthralled in a new book.
